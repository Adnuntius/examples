Expo9 Ad Served by DFP

The following Expo9 Ad is being served by DFP:

  <script type="text/javascript"></script>
  <script type="text/javascript" src="//tags.expo9.exponential.com/tags/Vetogatecom/ROS/tags.js"></script>

No ad will appear here because this ad is not being served on the Vetogate site. Nevertheless, the Expo9 ad content is delivered.

What you will find is that about 30% of the time, the Expo9 ad content mistimes when it uses document.write and the iframe content empties.

To see the ad content, right-click within the green border and click Inspect and then go into the iframe document. If it's empty, that's the Expo9 document.write code emptying the iframe.
